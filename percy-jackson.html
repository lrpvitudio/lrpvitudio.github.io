Percy Jackson Series

The Percy Jackson series is written by Rick Riordan. It follows the story of Percy Jackson, a demigod son of Poseidon, and his adventures with friends as they battle monsters and explore Greek mythology.

Books in the series include:

  • The Lightning Thief
  • The Sea of Monsters
  • The Titan's Curse
  • The Battle of the Labyrinth
  • The Last Olympian
