Heroes of Olympus Series

The Heroes of Olympus series is a spin-off from the Percy Jackson series. It tells the story of the next generation of demigods as they come together to stop the awakening of the earth goddess, Gaea.

Books in the series include:

  • The Lost Hero
  • The Son of Neptune
  • The Mark of Athena
  • The House of Hades
  • The Blood of Olympus
